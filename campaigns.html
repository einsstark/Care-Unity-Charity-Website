Campaings

At Care Unity, our campaings extends far beyond rhetoric; it's a commitment woven into every action we take.
We are dedicated to proactively alleviating suffering through concrete measures that make a difference. This includes operating shelters to provide refuge, nourishment, and emotional support to individuals affected by disasters of all scales.
We play a pivotal role in safeguarding public health by supplying approximately 40% of the nation's blood, ensuring that hospitals and medical facilities have the vital resources they need to save lives in times of emergency.
Moreover, our commitment to community resilience extends to education and empowerment. Through our programs, we teach life-saving skills that equip individuals with the knowledge and tools to respond effectively to emergencies, potentially saving countless lives in the process.
